Grounded. Gritty. Unapologetically Kriti. From her debut in ‘Heropanti’ to becoming one of Hindi cinema’s most versatile actresses, Kriti Sanon (@kritisanon) has carved her own path. Balancing mass entertainers with critically acclaimed performances, she continues to evolve with purpose. “I don’t think I was ever chasing fame; I am always chasing excellence in whatever I do,” she says.
